Collaboration // is a challenge for actors. An actor wants to force another actor to behave in a pre-planned manner, but acting must be spontaneous and reactive to the situation. We should practice the mindset, emotions, and scene objective, not the exact script pattern. Let that happen in real-time. The final performance is not what you practiced, it’s the real one happening live.
