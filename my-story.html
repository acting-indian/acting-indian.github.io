- MY STORY -
ACTING INDIAN

⭐️ ACTOR & FILMMAKER

- I was extremely shy and dramatic in my childhood.
- I surprised my mother once, by planning an act of my death.
- But I also faced a day when I saw my mother dying in hospital.
- Even before this, I lost my father, I had fear of death for years.
- I used to sing bollywood songs to entertain people around me.
- I always loved experimenting silently with whatever I had.
- I was always busy making gadgets, speaker boxes to voice command automations.
- I wanted to learn everything but was never interested in books.
- My mother encouraged me for singing, only 2 times I performed in school.
- In academics I was below average first, then good, then finally worst.
- I was equally interested in being Artist and Engineer like personality.
- I struggled a lot to follow career like Bill Gates and Steve Jobs.
- I had life experiences, few amazing and lot of painful.
- I always had courage and unbreakable commitment but still lived in chaos.
- I lost my software engineering job during covid pandemic.
- The job which I didn't wanted to do but had no financial choice.
- Finally I decided to leave technical dreams and try being and artist.
- I already lived a very complex life which had all the phases.
- Now suddenly saying that I am an Artist is another chaos in reality.
- I tried running youtube channels in almost all falvours.
- Gadget reviews, movie review, news, comedy, tutorials, shorts, reels.
- Nothing worked. It made me realize that I was better before.
- Then again in place of leaving all this I decided to attempt more bigger idea.
- I said, I am a Superstar. I am the best Actor and Filmmaker of all times.
- I always had almost no choice in my life.
- and even trying hard I was not able to change so called written life flow.
- So this time I didn't tried to find compromising solution.
- This time I reacted with a very big chaos to my chaotic life.
- I never performed at any stage as an actor ever, and I am a supertar actor?
- I made few youtube videos, learned video editing and I am a great filmmaker?
- NO. I changed my sim, changed email, i have new name "Acting Indian"
- I am learning from free online course videos, youtube tutorials
- I am learning by watching movies, OTT content
- Whatever I am trying to do now is seriously looks imposible even to me.
- I still have lifestyle with boundaries of responsibilities which I must not cross.
- But at least I should dream big and try to take it as close as possible.
- I believe Acting and Filmmaking is the freedom which I always deserved.
